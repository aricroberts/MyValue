1. What we collect (and don't)

The short version: The numbers, estimates, and personal details you enter into receipts. tools stay in your browser's memory only. We do not store, transmit, or log your calculation data.

receipts. is operated by AricCivicLabs.Org. Our tools are designed around a strict privacy model: your inputs never leave your device unless you explicitly export or copy them yourself.

What we do not collect
  • Salary figures, ROI estimates, or any financial data you enter into our calculators
  • Employer names, job titles, or company information
  • Grant data, program metrics, or donor information entered into nonprofit tools
  • Team data, project values, or business financial data
  • Any calculation results or generated documents
What we may collect
  • Contact form submissions — if you reach out via our contact form, we receive your name, email, and message via Formspree
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe; see Section 3
  • Basic analytics — aggregate, anonymized page visit data; see Section 4

2. How your data works in our tools

All receipts. calculators, coaches, and tools operate entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Here is exactly what that means:

  • Calculation dataJavaScript memory (RAM) only. Your salary figures, ROI estimates, and financial inputs exist only while the page is open. Closing or refreshing the tab destroys them permanently.
  • sessionStorageUsed only to remember your unlock state (whether you've paid to access premium features) within the current browser tab. Cleared automatically when you close the tab. Never contains your calculation data.
  • localStorageUsed in two limited cases: (1) to restore form field values you entered in a previous visit, as a convenience feature, and (2) by InAble to persist your subscription unlock state across sessions. The Destroy button clears all localStorage entries for the tool.
  • Cookies (tool data)Not used for tool data. See Section 4 for analytics.
  • Server transmissionYour calculation data is never sent to our servers. The exception is AI coach inputs — see below.
  • Destroy functionEvery tool includes a "Destroy session" button that clears all JavaScript variables, sessionStorage, and localStorage for that tool and resets the UI immediately.
  • AI coach callsWhen you use AI coaching features, the text you type is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. This is a live transmission to a third-party service. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle that data. See Section 5 for details.
  • ExportsJSON and PDF exports are generated client-side only. Files are created in your browser and downloaded directly to your device. We never receive a copy.
Important: AI coaching features transmit your inputs to Anthropic. When you use built-in coaching or AI analysis features in any receipts. or AricCivicLabs tool, the text you type is sent over the internet to Anthropic's Claude API. This is a live transmission to a third-party service — not local computation.

What this means: Anthropic receives the content of your coaching prompts. Anthropic's current API usage policies prohibit using those inputs to train AI models, but their data retention and processing practices are governed by their own privacy policy, not ours. We do not control what Anthropic does with data once transmitted.

Our recommendation: Avoid including highly sensitive personal identifiers (Social Security numbers, full legal names combined with employer details, specific financial account information) in coaching prompts. The tools are designed so that role-based or approximated inputs ("a software engineer in healthcare saving 3 hours per week") work just as well as specific ones.

If your role involves access to confidential, proprietary, or trade-secret information belonging to an employer or third party, you are solely responsible for ensuring you have authorization to share that information with third-party AI services before entering it into any coaching prompt. When in doubt, use approximations.
