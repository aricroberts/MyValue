Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Effective immediately

Plain-English summary: InAble helps you navigate social situations. When you type a description of your situation, that text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate suggestions — this is a live transmission to a third-party service. We don't sell your data, we don't store your conversations on our end, and if you pay for a subscription your unlock state is saved in your browser's localStorage so the app stays unlocked across sessions on that device. The mic only ever captures your own voice while you're actively holding a button.

1. Who we are

InAble is a social coaching tool for introverts, built by AricCivicLabs.Org and available at inable.aricciviclabs.org. For privacy questions, contact us at hello@aricciviclabs.org.

2. What data we collect and why

Text you type or dictate
When you use InAble's Coach, Prep, Debrief, or Decoder features, you describe your social situation in your own words. This text is sent to Anthropic's API to generate suggestions. We transmit it; we do not store it on our servers beyond the duration of your request.

Microphone input
The mic button in InAble captures only your own voice, only while you are actively pressing and holding the button. It does not listen to ambient conversation, does not record other people, and does not run in the background. The captured audio is converted to text locally by your device's speech recognition engine (Web Speech API) and only the resulting text — not audio — is sent to InAble's servers.

Unlock state (localStorage)
If you purchase an InAble subscription, your unlock state is saved in your browser's localStorage so the app remains unlocked across sessions on that device. This is a key (e.g., inable_monthly) with a value of "1" — it contains no personal or financial information. You can clear it at any time using the in-app Destroy / Reset button or your browser's developer tools.

Account information
InAble does not currently require or create user accounts. Unlock state is managed via browser localStorage only. If account-based authentication is introduced in a future version, this policy will be updated before that feature ships.

3. How we use your data

  • To generate AI-powered suggestions in response to your inputs via Anthropic's Claude API
  • To maintain your unlock state in localStorage if you purchase a subscription
  • To process payments and send receipts via Stripe
  • To respond to contact form submissions via Formspree

We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers.

4. Third parties who receive your data

Anthropic, PBC
The text you type into InAble's coaching features is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. This is a live transmission to a third-party service — not local processing. Anthropic's API usage policies prohibit using API inputs to train models, but their data retention practices are governed by their own privacy policy. Review it at anthropic.com/privacy.

Stripe, Inc.
Purchases are processed by Stripe. When you check out, Stripe receives your name, email address, billing address, and payment card details. We receive only a payment confirmation and your email (used to send a receipt). Stripe's processing is governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy.

Vercel
InAble is hosted on Vercel's infrastructure. Standard request logs (IP address, timestamp, URL) are retained per Vercel's standard data retention policies. Review at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not use any advertising networks, social tracking pixels, or data brokers.

5. Data retention

Your conversation inputs are not stored by InAble after a response is delivered — they are transmitted to Anthropic and then discarded on our end. Your localStorage unlock state persists on your device until you clear it or use the Destroy button. Stripe retains your payment and transaction records per their own policies. Formspree retains contact form submissions per their policies.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the following rights:

European Economic Area and UK (GDPR / UK GDPR)

  • Right of access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to restrict processing — limit how we use your data in certain circumstances

Our lawful basis for processing your conversation inputs is contract performance (providing the service you requested). Our basis for anonymised analytics is legitimate interests.

California (CCPA / CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of sale (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Voice data captured by the mic button is classified as sensitive personal information under CPRA. It is processed locally on your device and only the text transcript is transmitted. We do not retain audio recordings.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@aricciviclabs.org. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and local storage

InAble does not use cookies. Unlock state for paid subscribers is stored in your browser's localStorage, not cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics cookies.

8. Children

InAble is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. The Anthropic API key used for the InAble landing page demo is stored in a server-side environment variable and is never exposed to your browser. The main InAble app (inable.html) makes direct API calls from your browser in environments where an API key is injected by the hosting context (e.g., Claude.ai widget). We conduct periodic security reviews.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will notify you by email (if you have an account) or by posting a notice in the app at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or complaints: hello@aricciviclabs.org

If you are in the EEA and are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
