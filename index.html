Important disclosure

Directional tools, not professional advice

Estimates, not guarantees

All calculations are based on self-reported inputs and publicly available benchmarks. Results are directional estimates intended to support your thinking — not certified valuations or guarantees.

Not professional advice

receipts. is not a substitute for licensed financial, legal, accounting, or career counseling. Before major decisions, consult a qualified professional who can review your specific situation.

Verify with reality

AI-generated outputs may not match your local market, employer, or funder. Always cross-check results against current, authoritative sources before using them in high-stakes conversations.

Your data stays with you

Nothing you enter is stored, transmitted, or logged. All inputs live only in your browser tab and are destroyed when you leave. Exports are generated entirely on your device.

By using receipts., you acknowledge that outputs are for planning and preparation only. AricCivicLabs assumes no liability for decisions made based on these tools.
