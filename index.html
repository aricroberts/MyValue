Important disclosure

Directional tools, not professional advice

Estimates, not guarantees

All calculations are based on self-reported inputs and publicly available benchmarks. Results are directional estimates intended to support your thinking — not certified valuations, financial projections, or guarantees of outcome.

Not professional advice

receipts. is not a substitute for licensed financial, legal, accounting, or career counseling. Before making major compensation, hiring, investment, or retirement decisions, consult a qualified professional who can review your specific situation.

Verify with reality

AI-generated outputs — including salary benchmarks, grant language, negotiation scripts, and impact narratives — reflect patterns in training data and may not match your local market, employer, or funder. Always cross-check results against current, authoritative sources.

Your data stays with you

Nothing you enter is stored, transmitted, or logged. All inputs live only in your browser tab and are destroyed when you leave. Any exported files contain only your own self-reported data and are generated entirely on your device.

By using receipts., you acknowledge that outputs are for planning and preparation purposes only. AricCivicLabs assumes no liability for decisions made based on these tools. Use receipts. as a starting point — then do your reality check.
